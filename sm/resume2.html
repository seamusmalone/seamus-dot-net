Goal
I am searching for a tenure track position teaching performance and/or new media in a progressive department that embraces my focus on the relationship between theory and practice and socially engaged art. I would also welcome opportunities to teach art theory or modern and contemporary art history, especially courses focused on the areas of my research in critical theory and its relationship to art practice.
Other studio courses I am capable of teaching include, painting, sculputure, graphic design, computer graphics and dtp, web and user interface design and programming, digital imaging, film appreciation, as well as core 2d, 3d and time arts curriculae.
