Candyflipping
Candyflipping is the story of love, friendship and betrayal in a sugar-coated urban wasteland: the underground rave scene of New York. It is about the attempts we make to create a space where we can begin again, to re-invent hope. It is about the difference between the trap of escapism and the power of imagination. It is about the cold fact that freedom and meaning in our lives cannot be achieved alone.
