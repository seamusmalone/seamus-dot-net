space returns null value
   space returns is a web based project that contemplates the unseen spaces of production of digital technology. As an artist and a technology worker, this project attempts to bridge both worlds. It contests the ideology that the internet transcends space and embodyment, that as they say, "there's no there there." The project attempts to use the internet based art projects to address the internet itself, not taking it as a "transparent" medium.
    This series of images "space returns null value" from the project were taken in empty Silicon Valley office space after the burst of the "internet bubble." The title of the work of course describes the economic relation of the crash of commercial real estate following the economic as though it were a line of code.
